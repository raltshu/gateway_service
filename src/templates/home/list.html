This site allows you to predict a price a daimond based on a set of given features.

It is based on the Seaborn's Diamonds dataset.

The prediction model, research and conclusion can be reviewed on my Goolge Collab Notebook here 

In a nutshell the model is mainly based on RandomForest Regressor, however in an effort to reduce the RMSPE I cretaed few models based on the diamond's cut and then combined the result with an overall regression mode to get the best results.
